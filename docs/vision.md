# Vision

## What it is
- A decision orchestration system that turns context, constraints, and commitments into a short, time-bounded plan.
- A stateful lifecycle for daily check-ins, today plans, history, and weekly reviews.
- A source of stable, auditable decisions, not an ever-growing task list.

## What it is not
- Not a general productivity or task management app.
- Not an assistant that performs actions on your behalf.
- Not a medical, legal, or financial advisor.
- Not a surveillance or employee monitoring tool.

## Target users (high-pressure contexts)
- Operators and founders making rapid trade-offs with limited time.
- On-call or incident-response leaders coordinating competing priorities.
- Caregivers and managers balancing multiple dependent obligations.
- Users with high consequence decisions and frequent context switches.

## Value proposition (decision orchestration, not productivity)
- Reduces decision fatigue by compressing choices into the top priorities that fit the day.
- Enforces constraints (time, commitments, recovery) so the plan is realistic.
- Produces a clear, stable plan with rationale rather than a growing backlog.

## Pricing hypotheses
- Individual subscription for personal decision orchestration.
- Team plan with shared visibility into priorities and weekly summaries.
- Enterprise plan with audit logs, controls, and SLA support.
- Usage-based add-on for heavy engine or export volume.

## Ethical boundaries
- Preserve user autonomy; recommendations are optional and overrideable.
- No manipulative nudges, dark patterns, or pressure tactics.
- No sensitive or high-stakes advice outside the product scope.
- No covert data collection; all tracking is explicit and purpose-bound.
