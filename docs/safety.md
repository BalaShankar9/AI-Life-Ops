# Safety

AI Life Ops provides deterministic decision support. It is not medical, mental health, or legal advice.

## What the app is
- A structured planning system for high-pressure days.
- A deterministic engine that prioritizes risk reduction and feasibility.

## What the app is not
- Not a medical, mental health, or crisis service.
- Not therapy, diagnosis, or legal advice.

## Safe language rules
- Use calm, neutral phrasing; no urgency inflation.
- Avoid diagnosis or clinical labels.
- Avoid directives that could cause harm; use supportive, opt-in language.
- Frame outcomes as expected ranges or variability, not guarantees.

## Crisis-safe policy
If user notes contain high-risk self-harm language, the engine:
- Flags crisis risk as high.
- Pauses normal planning and returns a single priority: reach immediate support.
- Adds a safety notice encouraging immediate support.

## Emergency resources (generic)
If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number or contact a trusted person right now.
